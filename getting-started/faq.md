# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Ekgrona?

Ekgrona is a unified productivity platform that combines essential tools and applications into a single workspace.

Core features include:

* Dashboard
* File Manager
* Task Manager
* Applications
* Plugins
* Utility Tools

---

## Is Ekgrona free to use?

Available plans and pricing may vary depending on the platform's current offerings.

Refer to the official website for the latest information.

---

## Do I need to install software?

No.

Ekgrona is designed to be accessible through a modern web browser.

Additional applications or mobile clients may be available in the future.

---

## How do I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page.

Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

---

## Why can't I access my account?

Possible reasons include:

* Incorrect login credentials
* Unverified email address
* Temporary account restrictions
* Service interruptions

Verify your information and try again.

---

## How do I organize my files?

Use the File Manager to:

* Create folders
* Upload files
* Move items
* Rename files
* Delete unwanted content

---

## How do I manage tasks?

Use the Task Manager to:

* Create tasks
* Set priorities
* Track progress
* Mark tasks as completed

---

## What are Applications?

Applications are installable modules that extend the functionality of your workspace.

They allow users to customize Ekgrona according to their needs.

---

## What are Plugins?

Plugins provide integrations and additional functionality that enhance the platform.

Plugins may connect external services or add new capabilities.

---

## Is my data secure?

Ekgrona is designed with security and privacy in mind.

Users should also:

* Use strong passwords
* Keep credentials private
* Enable available security features

---

## How can I report a bug?

Use the official support or feedback channels provided by the platform.

When reporting issues, include:

* Steps to reproduce the problem
* Screenshots if available
* Browser and device information

---

## How can I request a feature?

Feature requests can be submitted through the platform's feedback channels.

User feedback plays an important role in improving Ekgrona.
