# Workspace Setup
This guide will help you configure your Ekgrona workspace and prepare it for daily use.

---

## Access Your Workspace
After signing in, you will be directed to your Dashboard.

The Dashboard serves as the central location for accessing all available tools and applications.

---

## Complete Your Profile
Before using the platform, review and update your account information.

Recommended actions:
* Add your name
* Upload a profile picture
* Verify your email address
* Review account settings

Keeping your profile information up to date helps maintain account security and improves your overall experience.

---

## Configure Your Workspace
Your workspace can be customized to fit your workflow.

Depending on available features, you may be able to:
* Rearrange dashboard widgets
* Customize layouts
* Configure preferences
* Adjust appearance settings

---

## Organize Your Files
Use the File Manager to create an organized structure for your files.

Recommended approach:
Projects/
Documents/
Images/
Others/

A consistent folder structure makes files easier to locate and manage.

---

## Set Up Task Management
Create categories or workflows that match your needs.

Examples:
* Personal Tasks
* Work Tasks
* Study Tasks
* Project Tasks

Regularly updating task status helps keep your workspace organized.

---

## Explore Applications
Applications extend the capabilities of your workspace.

Review available applications and install those that support your workflow.

Applications can be added or removed at any time.

---

## Explore Utility Tools
Utility Tools provide quick access to productivity-focused features.

Experiment with available tools and determine which are most useful for your daily activities.

---

## Workspace Best Practices
To get the most from Ekgrona:
* Keep files organized
* Review tasks regularly
* Remove unused content
* Explore new applications
* Keep account information updated

---
