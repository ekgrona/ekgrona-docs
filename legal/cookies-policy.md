# Cookies Policy

Last Updated: May 30, 2026

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function properly and improve user experience.

---

## How Ekgrona Uses Cookies

Cookies may be used to:

* Maintain user sessions
* Remember preferences
* Improve platform performance
* Enhance security
* Analyze platform usage

---

## Types of Cookies

### Essential Cookies

Required for core platform functionality.

### Preference Cookies

Remember user settings and preferences.

### Analytics Cookies

Help understand how users interact with the platform.

---

## Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow users to manage, block, or delete cookies through browser settings.

Disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.
